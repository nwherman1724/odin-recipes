Pizza

Melted cheese and peperoni upon a bed of tomato sauce and a foundation of thick crispy crust!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Peperoni

Steps

  1. Spread out dough into square pan.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and top with peperoni.
  4. Bake in the oven.
