Peanut Butter Balls

Peanut buttery goodness mixed with rice krispies and dipped in chocolate!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Rice Krispies
  • Confections Sugar
  • Chocolate
  • Melting Wax

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Roll into balls.
  3. Freeze overnight.
  4. Melt chocolate and wax in double boiler.
  5. Dip PB balls.
  6. Freeze overnight.
